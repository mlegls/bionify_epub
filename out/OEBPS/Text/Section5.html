Authors’ Note

This book is the companion volume to Anti-Oedipus (paperback ed., University of Minnesota Press, 1983). 

Together they make up Capitalism and Schizophrenia.

It is composed not of chapters but of “plateaus.” We will try to explain why later on (and also why the texts are dated). To a certain extent, these plateaus may be read independently of one another, except the conclusion, which should be read at the end.
